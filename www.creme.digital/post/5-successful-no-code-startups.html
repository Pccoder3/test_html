No-Code Startups Are Reaping Major Benefits

No-code tools have helped countless entrepreneurs build their products without technical skills. As a result, businesses are reaping significant benefits. By enabling rapid market testing and profound cost and time savings, no-code solutions allow businesses to thrive. In this blog, we will outline five successful no-code startups, illustrating the power and capabilities you can achieve with this development method.

‍

Top 5 Successful No-Code Startups

  1. Dividend Finance
  2. Comet
  3. BloomTech
  4. Teal
  5. Plato

‍

1. Dividend Finance

Website: Dividendfinance.com

‍

Company Description: Founded in 2013, Dividend Finance is a leading fin-tech platform established to create a more efficient and sustainable world by connecting homeowners, customer-centric contractors, and financial investors. In doing so, they create partnerships that improve the outcome for everyone involved. Today, Dividend Finance is a leading provider of financing solutions for renewable energy, energy-efficient upgrades, and general home improvement to property owners. This service allows customers to obtain financing through a comprehensive set of options, while experiencing a seamless and easy process.

‍

Key Facts & Figures:

  • Attracted investments of over $384 million
  • Has processed over $1 billion in loans
  • Various industry awards

‍

Use of No-Code: Bubble

‍

2. Comet

Website: Comet.co

‍

Company Description: Launched in 2016 by three college roommates, Comet is an online marketplace that matches tech and data freelancers to companies seeking help in specific roles or projects. Comet strives to satisfy both sides of the operation: relevant and prompt skills for companies, security and inspiring projects for freelancers. With over 10,000 qualified and available freelancers, and a loaded clientele, they have successfully achieved this mission. Powered by AI, Comet can intelligently detect and qualify prospective matches, enhancing the user experience and building an elite community.

‍

Key Facts & Figures:

  • Attracted investments of over €17 million
  • Supported 1,000+ clients
  • Monthly recurring revenue of $800k
  • Clients include 40% of the CAC40 & SBF 120

‍

Use of No-Code: Bubble

‍

3. BloomTech

Website: Bloomtech.com

‍

Company Description: Founded in 2017, BloomTech strives to fix today's broken education systems. This platform allows students to learn new skills and get a valuable education without paying upfront tuition. Instead of following traditional methods, BloomTech utilizes an income share agreement model, where students can pay back their tuition once BloomTech helps them secure a job. This platform aims to assist individuals in getting a well-paying job, faster, and with less risk. Through this unique approach, BloomTech teaches the skills employers want and offers constant support throughout the job search process.

‍

Key Facts & Figures:

  • Attracted investments of over $122 million
  • 3,000+ users
  • Median income boost of $27,500 for graduates
  • 90% job placement rate for graduates

‍

Use of No-Code: Webflow, Typeform, Airtable, and Zapier

‍

4. Teal

Website: Tealhq.com

‍

Company Description: Teal is a career growth platform that gives individuals the tools, skills, and recommendations needed to accelerate their careers, starting with the job search. This platform compassionately guides people to organize this process, implementing a wide variety of effective features. From resume building, application tracking, profile analyzing, and many more advanced tools, Teal empowers and energizes your professional career. Through their no-code platform, Teal is helping users drive their careers with confidence and purpose, encouraging them to invest in themselves and providing support along the way.

‍

Key Facts & Figures:

  • Attracted investments of over $11 million
  • 11,000+ users

‍

Use of No-Code: Bubble, Webflow, Airtable, Zapier, HubSpot

‍

5. Plato

Website: Platohq.com

‍

Company Description: Founded in 2018, Plato is the world’s largest mentorship platform for engineers, connecting such individuals to their needed resources. By engaging users in a 1-1 mentorship program, immersing them in cohort-based learning, and granting access to useful and exclusive content, Plato provides an invaluable stepping stone to career acceleration. The mentors available on Plato’s platform have extensive management experience, stemming from top technology companies like Google, Facebook, Netflix, Spotify, Lyft, Slack, and more.

‍

Key Facts & Figures:

  • Attracted investments of over $13 million
  • 1,500+ senior engineering leaders and executives

‍

Use of No-Code: Bubble
